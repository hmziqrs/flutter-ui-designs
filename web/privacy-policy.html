Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how "Flutter UI Designs" collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. Our app uses third-party services including Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Performance Monitoring, and Google AdMob.

Information Collection and Use

While using our app, certain data may be collected by our third-party service providers:

  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us understand how users interact with our app
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports to help improve app stability
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: Monitors app performance metrics
  • Google AdMob: Delivers personalized advertisements

Types of Data Collected

The following information may be collected:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data
  • Advertising identifiers

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google Firebase and Google AdMob services. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
